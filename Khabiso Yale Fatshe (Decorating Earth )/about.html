Our Story

Khabiso Ya Lefatshe was founded by Banele Ndlovu and Tebogo Mkhwanazi in 2021 after high school. Incorporated in 2024, the business continues to grow with a vision to create a cleaner, greener South Africa.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to expand across Gauteng and build a recycling manufacturing plant to combat imports and reduce carbon footprint. Our mission is to empower communities, fight poverty, and protect the environment through sustainable eco-friendly practices.
